<p><p>Process gas centrifugal compressors are essential in various industrial applications, and they come in two main market types: single-stage and multi-stage compressors. Single-stage compressors are designed for lower pressure applications, offering simplicity and lower costs. In contrast, multi-stage compressors can achieve higher pressure ratios by utilizing multiple impellers, making them suitable for more demanding processes. The choice between these types depends on the specific pressure requirements and efficiency needs of the gas compression application.</p></p>
