<p><p>The Airport Terminal Sign Market includes **Static** and **Dynamic** types. **Static signs** are fixed, displaying permanent information like directions, gate numbers, or restroom locations. They are cost-effective and reliable but lack flexibility. **Dynamic signs** are digital, updating in real-time to show flight statuses, gate changes, or delays. They enhance passenger experience with adaptability but require higher maintenance and investment. Both types are essential for efficient airport operations, balancing cost, functionality, and real-time communication needs.</p></p>
