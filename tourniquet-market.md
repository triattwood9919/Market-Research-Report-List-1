<p><ul><li>Latex Tourniquet</li><li>TPE Tournique</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tourniquet market includes two primary types: latex tourniquets and TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are widely used due to their elasticity and strength, providing effective vascular occlusion for medical procedures. However, they may cause allergic reactions in some patients. In contrast, TPE tourniquets offer a latex-free alternative, appealing to those with allergies. They are also lightweight and durable, making them suitable for a variety of healthcare settings. Both types are essential for managing bleeding and ensuring patient safety during medical interventions.</p></p>
