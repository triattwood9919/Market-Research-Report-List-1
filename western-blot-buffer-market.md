<p><strong>The Western Blot Buffer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Running Buffer</li><li>Transfer Buffer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Western Blot buffer market comprises several types, including running buffer, transfer buffer, and others. Running buffers, typically used in electrophoresis, facilitate the migration of proteins through the gel matrix, ensuring efficient separation. Transfer buffers are essential for transferring separated proteins from the gel to a membrane, maintaining protein integrity. Other buffers may include blocking solutions and dilution buffers, which are crucial for minimizing non-specific binding and optimizing detection in later analysis stages. Each plays a vital role in Western Blotting procedures.</p></p>
