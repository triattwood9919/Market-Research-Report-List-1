<p><strong>The Friction Welding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LinearFrictionWelding</li><li>RotaryFrictionWelding</li><li>StirFrictionWelding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The friction welding market includes three primary types: Linear Friction Welding, Rotary Friction Welding, and Stir Friction Welding. </p><p>Linear Friction Welding involves the oscillation of one workpiece against another to generate heat, ideal for small parts and high-strength applications. Rotary Friction Welding uses rotational motion to create a forge-welded joint, commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries. Stir Friction Welding combines rotational and axial motion, providing excellent material flow, making it suitable for thick materials and complex geometries in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
