<p><p>The cathode binder market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based binders. Water-based binders are preferred due to their environmentally friendly properties and lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions. They promote a safer working environment and are compatible with various cathode materials. Conversely, solvent-based binders are favored for their superior adhesion and performance in high-temperature applications, although they may pose environmental and health risks due to higher VOC levels. Both types cater to different industrial needs and preferences.</p></p>
