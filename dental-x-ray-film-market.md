<p><p>The dental X-ray film market is primarily categorized into D-speed and Ultra-speed X-ray films. D-speed film is a slower film requiring longer exposure times, resulting in high-resolution images, making it suitable for various diagnostic applications. In contrast, Ultra-speed film offers faster exposure, capturing images with reduced radiation dose, enhancing patient safety and comfort. Both types serve essential functions in dental diagnostics, catering to different needs in clinical environments while balancing image quality and exposure efficiency.</p></p>
