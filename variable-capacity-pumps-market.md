<p><ul><li>Variable Displacement Piston Pumps</li><li>Variable Displacement Vane Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The variable capacity pumps market consists primarily of variable displacement piston pumps and variable displacement vane pumps. Variable displacement piston pumps adjust the flow rate by varying the stroke length of pistons, providing efficient fluid handling in diverse applications. In contrast, variable displacement vane pumps use movable vanes within a rotor to change the displacement, allowing for smoother operation and better efficiency. Both types serve industrial and hydraulic systems, offering flexibility and control in fluid management across various sectors.</p></p>
