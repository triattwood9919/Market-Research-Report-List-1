<p><strong>The TAXI Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AGM</li><li>EFB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The taxi battery market primarily features two types: AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and EFB (Enhanced Flooded Battery). AGM batteries are known for their superior performance, longer lifespan, and excellent deep cycling capabilities, making them ideal for demanding applications like taxis that frequently start and stop. In contrast, EFB batteries offer a cost-effective solution with decent performance and shorter cycle life, suitable for vehicles with less rigorous requirements. Both types cater to the evolving needs of the taxi industry, focusing on reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
