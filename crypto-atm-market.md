<p><ul><li>One Way</li><li>Two Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crypto ATMs can be classified into two main market types: one-way and two-way. One-way ATMs allow users to buy cryptocurrencies using cash or debit/credit cards but do not support selling cryptocurrencies back. Conversely, two-way ATMs enable users to both purchase cryptocurrencies and sell them for cash, offering more flexibility. This distinction impacts user experience and liquidity, with two-way ATMs providing a comprehensive service for those wishing to trade cryptocurrencies in either direction.</p></p>
