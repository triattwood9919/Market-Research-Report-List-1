<p><p>The concrete mold release agent market is categorized into oil-based and water-based types. Oil-based agents provide a robust barrier, reducing adhesion and enhancing surface finish, making them ideal for intricate molds. They offer longer-lasting performance but may pose environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. Water-based agents are more eco-friendly, easier to clean, and suitable for a variety of applications, especially where rapid curing is necessary. However, they may require more frequent applications and can be less effective in high-temperature scenarios.</p></p>
