<p><p>The bare copper conductor market is categorized into three types based on their mechanical properties: Soft Drawn, Medium Hard Drawn, and Hard Drawn. Soft Drawn conductors are highly ductile, allowing for easy bending and shaping, making them ideal for applications requiring frequent manipulation. Medium Hard Drawn conductors offer a balance of strength and flexibility, suitable for various electrical applications. Hard Drawn conductors are characterized by high tensile strength, making them suitable for applications where rigidity and durability are essential.</p></p>
