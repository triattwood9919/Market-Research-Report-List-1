<p><p>The toy drones market consists of three primary types: Fixed Wing, Rotor Type, and Flapping Wing. Fixed Wing drones resemble traditional aircraft, offering longer flight times and greater stability, ideal for outdoor use. Rotor Type drones, commonly known as quadcopters, have vertical take-off capabilities and are popular for indoor and recreational flying due to their agility and ease of control. Flapping Wing drones mimic birds or insects, focusing on realism and maneuverability, making them suitable for educational purposes and advanced hobbyists.</p></p>
