<p><p>Continuous Blood Glucose Monitoring (CGM) devices are categorized into two main types: wearable and implantable. Wearable devices, such as patches and smartwatches, provide real-time glucose readings and can be easily removed or replaced. Implantable devices, however, are surgically placed under the skin for continuous monitoring, offering long-term glucose tracking without the need for frequent replacements. Both types enhance diabetes management by providing consistent data, improving glycemic control, and allowing for timely interventions in blood sugar levels.</p></p>
