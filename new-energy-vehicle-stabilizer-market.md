<p><strong>The New Energy Vehicle Stabilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Hollow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) stabilizer market includes two main types: solid and hollow stabilizers. Solid stabilizers are made from a single piece of metal, offering high strength and durability, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Hollow stabilizers, constructed with a tubular design, are lighter, reducing vehicle weight and improving energy efficiency, which is crucial for NEVs. Both types enhance vehicle stability and handling, but hollow stabilizers are increasingly preferred in NEVs due to their weight-saving benefits and alignment with sustainability goals.</p></p>
