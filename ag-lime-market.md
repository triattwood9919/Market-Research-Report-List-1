<p><ul><li>Quicklime</li><li>Slaked Lime</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ag-Lime market primarily includes three types: Quicklime, Slaked Lime, and Agricultural Lime. Quicklime, produced by heating limestone, is used for soil stabilization and pH adjustment. Slaked Lime, formed by adding water to Quicklime, is more soluble and often applied in agricultural practices to improve soil quality. Agricultural Lime, a crushed limestone product, is widely used for neutralizing acidic soils and enhancing nutrient availability. Together, these lime types play crucial roles in agriculture and soil management.</p></p>
