<p><strong>The Badge Card Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet</li><li>Dye Sub</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The badge card printing machine market primarily features two types: inkjet and dye sublimation (dye sub). Inkjet machines utilize liquid ink ejected onto the card surface, providing vibrant colors and versatility for various print sizes. They are ideal for short runs and custom designs. Conversely, dye sublimation machines transfer dye onto the card using heat, creating high-resolution, durable prints that are resistant to wear and fading. This method is well-suited for bulk production of professional-quality badges.</p></p>
