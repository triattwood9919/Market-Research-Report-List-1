<p><p>The plastering mortars market comprises several key types, including premixed mounting mortars, premixed rush coats, and premixed plasters. Premixed mounting mortars are designed for bonding plaster to substrates effectively. Premixed rush coats serve as a quick-setting base layer, providing better adhesion and surface quality for subsequent finishes. Premixed plasters are ready-to-use formulations that offer smooth finishes for interior or exterior surfaces. Each type enhances efficiency and ease of application in construction and renovation projects.</p></p>
