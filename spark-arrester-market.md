<p><p>Spark arresters are essential devices designed to prevent the ignition of flammable materials by capturing and extinguishing sparks. In the market, they are classified into several types: </p><p>1. **Centrifugal Type**: This type uses centrifugal force to separate sparks from exhaust gases. </p><p>2. **Screen Type**: It employs a mesh or screen to physically block sparks, allowing gases to pass while trapping ignitable particles.  </p><p>3. **Other**: This category includes various innovative designs, such as electronic or hybrid models that enhance spark suppression efficiency.</p></p>
