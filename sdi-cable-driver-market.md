<p><p>The SDI cable driver market is categorized into HD-SDI, UHD-SDI, and SD-SDI types, each serving specific video resolution needs. UHD-SDI caters to ultra-high-definition formats, delivering 4K and beyond, suitable for advanced broadcasting applications. SD-SDI, with standard-definition capabilities, is used for legacy systems. Each type ensures reliable signal transmission with minimal latency, crucial for professional video production and distribution environments.</p></p>
