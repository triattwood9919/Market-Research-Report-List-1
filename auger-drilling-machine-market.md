<p><strong>The Auger Drilling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Flight Augers</li><li>Bucket Augers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auger Drilling Machine market primarily features Continuous Flight Augers and Bucket Augers. Continuous Flight Augers are equipped with a spiral blade that allows for continuous drilling without needing to remove soil after each rotation, making them efficient for deep drilling in cohesive soils. In contrast, Bucket Augers are designed with a bucket at the bottom to scoop out soil, ideal for various soil conditions and applications such as sampling or excavation. Both types cater to diverse drilling needs in construction and geotechnical projects.</p></p>
