<p><ul><li>Two-phase Decanter Centrifuge</li><li>Three-phase Decanter Centrifuge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal decanter centrifuges are classified into two main types based on separation capabilities: two-phase and three-phase decanter centrifuges. Two-phase decanter centrifuges separate solid-liquid mixtures, efficiently removing solids from liquids in industries like food processing and wastewater treatment. In contrast, three-phase decanter centrifuges separate solid-liquid-liquid mixtures, effectively isolating two immiscible liquids along with solids, commonly used in oil and chemical sectors. Both types enhance separation efficiency and product purity in various industrial processes.</p></p>
