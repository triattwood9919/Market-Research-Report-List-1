<p><p>The **Automotive Air Suspension Market** includes two main types: **Non-Electronically Controlled** and **Electronically Controlled** systems. Non-electronically controlled systems rely on manual or mechanical adjustments to regulate air pressure in the suspension, offering basic comfort and load-leveling capabilities. Electronically controlled systems, on the other hand, use sensors and electronic control units to automatically adjust air pressure, providing enhanced ride quality, stability, and adaptability to varying road conditions. Both types cater to different vehicle segments, balancing cost and performance.</p></p>
