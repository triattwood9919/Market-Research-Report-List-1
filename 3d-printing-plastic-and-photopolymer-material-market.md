<p><p>The 3D printing market for plastics and photopolymers encompasses various technologies tailored for different applications. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) involves layering thermoplastic filaments, ideal for prototyping. Stereolithography (SLA) uses UV light to cure liquid resin, producing high-precision parts. PolyJet Printing jets photopolymer droplets to create multi-material objects with fine details. Laser Sintering fuses powdered materials using lasers, enabling complex geometries. Digital Light Processing (DLP), similar to SLA, employs projectors for faster layer curing. Other methods include binder jetting and material jetting, expanding versatility.</p></p>
