<p><p>End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) therapy market includes three main treatment types: Hemodialysis, Peritoneal Dialysis, and Kidney Transplantation. Hemodialysis involves using a machine to filter blood, typically performed in clinics or at home. Peritoneal Dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdominal cavity to filter waste, allowing patients greater flexibility. Kidney Transplantation involves surgically replacing a failing kidney with a healthy one from a donor, providing a potential cure for ESRD. Each option plays a crucial role in managing this severe condition.</p></p>
