<p><strong>The Water Cooling Screw Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrodeless Adjustment</li><li>Sectional Adjustment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water cooling screw chiller market can be categorized into two main types based on their adjustment mechanisms: electrodeless adjustment and sectional adjustment. </p><p>Electrodeless adjustment chillers use variable frequency drives to optimize system performance dynamically, resulting in energy efficiency and reduced operational costs. On the other hand, sectional adjustment chillers feature discrete sections that allow for stepwise changes in capacity, offering simpler control but potentially less efficiency compared to their electrodeless counterparts. Both types serve various industrial and commercial cooling needs.</p></p>
