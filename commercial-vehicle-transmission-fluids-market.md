<p><ul><li>Manual Transmission Fluids</li><li>Automatic Transmission Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle transmission fluids are essential for optimizing vehicle performance and longevity. Manual transmission fluids are designed for vehicles with a manual gearbox, providing the necessary lubrication and friction characteristics to enable smooth gear shifting. In contrast, automatic transmission fluids cater to vehicles with automatic transmissions, featuring specialized additives that ensure proper hydraulic function, improved temperature stability, and enhanced wear protection. Both types play critical roles in maintaining efficient power transfer and overall drivetrain reliability in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
