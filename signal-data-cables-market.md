<p><p>The signal data cables market comprises three primary types: twisted pair cables, coaxial cables, and optical fibers. Twisted pair cables, made of copper, are widely used for networking and telecommunications due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Coaxial cables, also copper-based, excel at transmitting high-frequency signals, making them ideal for cable TV and internet distribution. Optical fibers utilize light to transmit data over long distances with minimal loss, offering high speed and bandwidth, essential for modern communication networks.</p></p>
