<p><p>The High Molecular Polymer Dispersant market can be categorized into two main types: anionic and cationic dispersants. Anionic dispersants carry a negative charge and are effective in stabilizing suspensions of negatively charged particles, commonly used in industries like paints and coatings. Cationic dispersants, on the other hand, possess a positive charge, making them suitable for applications involving positively charged particles, such as in textile and paper manufacturing. Each type is tailored for specific formulations and stability requirements in various industrial applications.</p></p>
