<p><p>The Automotive Blind Spot Detection market includes various sensor technologies to enhance driver safety. Radar sensors use radio waves to detect objects in blind spots, offering long-range accuracy. Ultrasonic sensors rely on sound waves for short-range detection, ideal for low-speed maneuvers. LIDAR sensors use laser pulses for precise, high-resolution object mapping, suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems. Other technologies, such as cameras or infrared sensors, provide additional detection methods. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing cost, range, and accuracy to improve vehicle safety and reduce collision risks.</p></p>
