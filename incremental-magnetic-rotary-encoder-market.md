<p><p>An Incremental Magnetic Rotary Encoder is a type of sensor used to convert the angular position of a shaft or axle into an analog or digital signal. It operates by utilizing magnetic fields to detect the rotation of a magnetic disk or rotor, providing precise feedback for motion control applications in various industries.
