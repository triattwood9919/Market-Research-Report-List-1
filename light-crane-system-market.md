<p><p>The Light Crane System market includes two primary types: Monorail and Double-rail systems. Monorail Light Crane Systems feature a single rail and are ideal for spaces with height constraints, providing flexibility in movement and minimizing the footprint. In contrast, Double-rail Light Crane Systems consist of two parallel rails, offering greater stability and load capacity, making them suitable for heavier lifting tasks. Both systems enhance efficiency in material handling by optimizing workflows in industrial environments.</p></p>
