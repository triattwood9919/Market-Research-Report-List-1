<p><p>The helicopter rotor blades market comprises two main types: Main Rotor Blades and Tail Rotor Blades. Main Rotor Blades are the primary lifting and propulsion components, generating lift and enabling forward, backward, and lateral movement. Tail Rotor Blades, mounted on the tail, counteract torque produced by the main rotor, ensuring stability and directional control. Both blade types are critical for flight performance, safety, and maneuverability, with designs tailored to specific helicopter models and operational requirements. The market focuses on durability, efficiency, and advanced materials for enhanced performance.</p></p>
