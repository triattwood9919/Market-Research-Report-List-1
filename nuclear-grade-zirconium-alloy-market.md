In contrast, powder zirconium is manufactured via techniques like milling and atomization, offering versatile applications in fuel fabrication and other nuclear components. Both forms are essential for ensuring optimal performance and safety in nuclear reactors due to their favorable properties and resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
