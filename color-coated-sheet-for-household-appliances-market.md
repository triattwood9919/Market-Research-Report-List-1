<p><p>Color coated sheets for household appliances are primarily categorized into two types: PCM (Pre-painted Cold Rolled Steel) and VCM (Vinyl Coated Metal). PCM features a painted finish that enhances aesthetics and corrosion resistance, making it ideal for appliances like refrigerators and washing machines. VCM, on the other hand, has a vinyl coating that offers superior durability and moisture resistance, providing an extra layer of protection against wear. Both types are essential for improving the longevity and visual appeal of household appliances.</p></p>
