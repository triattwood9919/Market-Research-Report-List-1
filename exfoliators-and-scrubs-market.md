<p><p>The exfoliators and scrubs market can be broadly categorized into two main types: natural and chemical. Natural exfoliators use organic ingredients such as sugar, salt, and ground grains to physically slough off dead skin cells, appealing to consumers seeking eco-friendly options. In contrast, chemical exfoliators utilize acids like alpha hydroxy acid (AHA) and beta hydroxy acid (BHA) to promote skin cell turnover and improve texture. Both categories address different consumer preferences, offering varied benefits and targeting diverse skin concerns.</p></p>
