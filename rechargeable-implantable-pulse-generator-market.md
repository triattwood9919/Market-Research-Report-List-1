<p><p>The rechargeable implantable pulse generator market is segmented into unipolar and multipolar types. Unipolar generators use a single electrode to deliver impulses, typically resulting in a broader current spread, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, multipolar generators utilize multiple electrodes, allowing for more precise control and targeted stimulation, enhancing therapeutic outcomes in complex conditions. Both types cater to different patient needs and preferences, driving advancements in implantable technologies and expanding treatment options in neuromodulation and pain management.</p></p>
