<p><p>The 3D glass testing market encompasses various testing solutions, including Two Shaft and Three Shaft systems. Two Shaft testing primarily evaluates the glass's rigidity and stress distribution through a dual-axis approach, allowing for efficient quality control of flat glass surfaces. In contrast, Three Shaft testing enhances precision by incorporating a third axis, enabling more detailed analysis of the glass's response to pressure and impacts. Both methods serve critical roles in ensuring that advanced glass products meet safety and performance standards.</p></p>
