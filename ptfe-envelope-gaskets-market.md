<p><p>PTFE envelope gaskets are specialized sealing components made from polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), a material known for its exceptional chemical resistance, thermal stability, and non-stick properties. These gaskets consist of a PTFE envelope filled with a soft filler material, such as compressed fiber or elastomers, providing a combination of flexibility and durability. They are widely used in industries such as chemical processing, oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, where resistance to corrosive chemicals, high temperatures, and pressure is critical.</p><p>The PTFE envelope gaskets market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand from end-use industries and the need for reliable sealing solutions in harsh operating conditions.
