<p><strong>The Structural Glass Curtain Walls Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unitised Type</li><li>Stick Built Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structural glass curtain walls are modern building exteriors that enhance aesthetics and energy efficiency. The market is primarily divided into two types: unitised and stick-built. Unitised systems consist of pre-manufactured panels assembled off-site, allowing for quicker installation and reduced labor costs. In contrast, stick-built systems are constructed on-site using individual components, offering flexibility in design but often requiring more time and labor. Both types are integral in contemporary architecture, catering to various construction needs and preferences.</p></p>
