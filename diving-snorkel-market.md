Semi-dry snorkels feature a splash guard to minimize water entry, improving comfort and usability. Dry snorkels have a valve system that keeps water out entirely, making them ideal for beginners and those seeking ease of use. Each type caters to different preferences and environments, enhancing the snorkeling experience.</p></p>
