<p><p>The COVID-19 test kits market encompasses various testing methodologies. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) provide quick results using blood, saliva, or other samples, enabling immediate decision-making. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) offers more detailed analysis by detecting specific antibodies or antigens, generally requiring laboratory processing. Neutralization Assays assess the functional ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, providing insights into immunity levels. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19, catering to different clinical and research needs.</p></p>
