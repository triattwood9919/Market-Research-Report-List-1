<p><p>Self-sealing tires feature a built-in layer that automatically seals punctures, preventing air loss and enhancing safety. In the market, diagonal tires (bias-ply) have crisscrossed layers, offering durability and load capacity but less flexibility and comfort. Radial tires, with perpendicular ply layers, provide better fuel efficiency, grip, and ride comfort due to their flexible sidewalls and stable tread. Both types can incorporate self-sealing technology, catering to diverse needs, with radial tires dominating modern markets due to superior performance and efficiency.</p></p>
