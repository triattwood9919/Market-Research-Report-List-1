<p><strong>The Municipal Tree Maintenance Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corrective Maintenance</li><li>Preventive Maintenance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Municipal tree maintenance services encompass two primary market types: corrective maintenance and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance focuses on addressing existing tree issues, such as removing hazardous limbs, treating diseases, or pruning damaged trees to ensure safety and health. Conversely, preventive maintenance aims to proactively manage tree health and growth through regular inspections, routine pruning, and planting new trees. This dual approach helps municipalities maintain urban green spaces, enhance public safety, and promote biodiversity while minimizing long-term costs.</p></p>
