Dye laser systems utilize organic compounds for precise wavelength control, making them effective for dermatological applications. Semiconductor laser systems are compact and energy-efficient, ideal for applications like laser therapy and phototherapy. Together, these systems enhance medical practices through advanced laser technology.</p></p>
