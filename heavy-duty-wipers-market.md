<p><p>The heavy-duty wipers market encompasses two primary types: dry wipers and pre-saturated wipers. Dry wipers are absorbent cloths designed for industrial cleaning, suitable for removing oils, dirt, and grease without any liquid solution. They offer versatility and durability for various applications. In contrast, pre-saturated wipers come imbued with cleaning solutions, making them ready to use right away for efficient surface cleaning and sanitation. Both types are essential in maintaining cleanliness in rigorous environments such as manufacturing, automotive, and maintenance sectors.</p></p>
