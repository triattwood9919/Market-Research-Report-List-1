<p><p>The food blanchers market includes various types such as belt blanchers, drum blanchers, and screw blanchers, each serving distinct food processing needs. Belt blanchers utilize a continuous conveyor system to evenly blanch products, enhancing efficiency. Drum blanchers feature rotating drums that facilitate thorough heating for bulk items. Screw blanchers employ a helical screw design to move and heat the food, ensuring consistent temperature control. These blanching methods are crucial for preserving color, flavor, and nutritional value in processed foods.</p></p>
