<p><p>The fluoro synthetic rubber market encompasses several key types, including fluorocarbon rubber, fluorosilicone rubber, and fluorinated phosphonitrile rubber. Fluorocarbon rubber is known for its exceptional chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making it ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. Fluorosilicone rubber combines the properties of silicone and fluorocarbon, offering resilience against fuels and solvents, suitable for demanding environments. Fluorinated phosphonitrile rubber features enhanced thermal stability and chemical resistance, used in specialized industrial applications such as seals and gaskets.</p></p>
