<p><ul><li>Multifilament</li><li>Monofilament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) filaments are categorized into two primary market types: multifilament and monofilament. Multifilament filaments consist of multiple fibers twisted together, providing excellent flexibility, strength, and resistance to harsh environments, making them suitable for applications in textiles and industrial products. In contrast, monofilament consists of a single, continuous filament that offers higher rigidity and durability, ideal for uses in automotive components and reinforced materials. Both types leverage PPS's inherent thermal and chemical resistance for specialized applications.</p></p>
