<p><p>The Car VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) Battery market includes two main types: AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and Gel Batteries. AGM batteries use fiberglass mats to absorb electrolyte, offering high power output, faster charging, and vibration resistance, making them ideal for start-stop vehicles and high-performance cars. Gel batteries use a silica-based gel electrolyte, providing deep-cycle capabilities, longer lifespan, and better performance in extreme temperatures, suited for applications requiring consistent power over time. Both are maintenance-free and spill-proof, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
