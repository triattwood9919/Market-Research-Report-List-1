<p><p>The Automotive Energy Storage System market comprises three key types: **flywheels**, **batteries**, and **supercapacitors**. Flywheels store energy mechanically, providing high power output and rapid discharge, ideal for performance applications. Batteries, particularly lithium-ion, dominate for energy density and efficiency, powering electric vehicles and hybrids. Supercapacitors excel in high power delivery and rapid charge/discharge cycles, making them suitable for regenerative braking and short-term energy storage. Together, these technologies support the evolving landscape of energy-efficient and sustainable automotive solutions.</p></p>
