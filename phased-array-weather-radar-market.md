<p><p>Phased Array Weather Radar technology offers two primary market types: Dual Polarized and Fully Polarized systems. Dual Polarized radars transmit and receive signals in both horizontal and vertical orientations, enhancing precipitation analysis and improving weather forecasts. Fully Polarized systems provide even more detailed data by utilizing a complete set of polarimetric measurements. This allows for advanced differentiation of precipitation types and better understanding of atmospheric conditions, ultimately leading to improved accuracy in severe weather warnings and meteorological studies.</p></p>
