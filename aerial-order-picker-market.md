<p><p>Aerial order pickers are categorized into electric and semi-electric markets based on their power source and operational capabilities. Electric order pickers operate entirely on electric power, offering high efficiency and low emissions, suitable for indoor environments. In contrast, semi-electric order pickers combine manual and electric operations, where the lift is powered but the movement is pedestrian. This provides a balance between mobility and power, making it ideal for locations with variable load requirements and tight spaces.</p></p>
