<p><strong>The Hemodialysis Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li><li>Double Pump Hemodialysis Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemodialysis machine market is primarily divided into two types: single pump and double pump machines. Single pump hemodialysis machines utilize one pump to manage both blood and dialysate flow, promoting simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for home use and smaller clinics. Double pump machines, on the other hand, leverage two separate pumps for each fluid, allowing for more precise flow control and higher efficiency, often preferred in hospital settings for patients with complex needs.</p></p>
