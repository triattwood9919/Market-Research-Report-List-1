<p><p>Nano power operational amplifiers (OpAmps) are classified into several market types based on channel configuration. Single-channel OpAmps are designed for applications requiring one amplification path, ideal for compact designs. Dual-channel OpAmps provide two amplification paths, enhancing functionality in space-constrained environments. Four-channel OpAmps support multiple signal processing in a single package, suitable for complex applications needing simultaneous operation. Each type caters to various needs in low-power applications, with unique benefits in performance, footprint, and versatility across industries like consumer electronics and healthcare.</p></p>
