<p><p>Induction lamps are a type of lighting technology that utilizes electromagnetic induction to produce light. These lamps consist of a sealed glass bulb filled with gas and a magnetic field, which excites the gas to emit ultraviolet light that then energizes a phosphor coating inside the bulb to produce visible light. Known for their energy efficiency and long lifespan, induction lamps are increasingly being adopted in commercial and industrial applications, such as street lighting, warehouses, and stadiums.</p><p>The Induction Lamps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.00% during the forecast period, driven by rising demand for energy-efficient lighting solutions and expanding environmental regulations favoring sustainable technologies.
