<p><strong>The Automotive Dimming Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outer Dimming Mirror</li><li>Inside Dimming Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive dimming mirrors enhance driving safety and comfort by reducing glare from headlights. **Outer dimming mirrors** are installed on the vehicle's exterior, typically on the side-view mirrors, and automatically adjust to minimize glare from trailing vehicles. **Inside dimming mirrors**, placed on the windshield, reduce glare from rear-facing headlights. Both types use electrochromic technology to darken when exposed to bright light, ensuring clear visibility. These mirrors are increasingly popular in modern vehicles, offering improved nighttime driving experiences and reducing eye strain for drivers.</p></p>
