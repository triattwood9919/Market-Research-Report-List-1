<p><strong>The Vehicle RFID Tag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle RFID tag market is primarily categorized into two types: active and passive tags. Active RFID tags are equipped with a battery, allowing them to transmit signals over longer distances and provide real-time data tracking. They are commonly used in high-traffic applications, such as fleet management. Conversely, passive RFID tags do not have a battery and rely on external signals to operate. They are cost-effective and widely used for vehicle identification and access control, especially in parking solutions.</p></p>
