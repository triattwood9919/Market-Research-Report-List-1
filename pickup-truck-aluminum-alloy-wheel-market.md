<p><p>The pickup truck aluminum alloy wheel market is categorized into three main types: casting, forging, and others. Casting involves pouring molten aluminum into a mold, creating intricate designs but may result in slightly lower strength. Forging, on the other hand, uses pressure to shape solid aluminum, yielding wheels with enhanced durability and performance. The "other" category includes alternative production methods like machining or hybrid processes, which may combine aspects of casting and forging to meet specific performance or aesthetic needs.</p></p>
