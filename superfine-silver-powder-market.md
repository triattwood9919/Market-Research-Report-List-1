<p><p>The Superfine Silver Powder market is categorized into three main types: spherical, flake, and others. Spherical silver powder features uniform, round particles, making it ideal for applications requiring high flowability and conductivity, such as electronics and additive manufacturing. Flake silver powder has a larger surface area and is often used in coatings, inks, and batteries due to its unique properties. The "others" category includes various unconventional forms that cater to niche applications, expanding the versatility of silver powder in various industries.</p></p>
