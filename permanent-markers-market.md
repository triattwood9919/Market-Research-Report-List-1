<p><p>The permanent markers market can be categorized into three main types: chisel tip, bullet tip, and jumbo chisel tip. </p><p>Chisel tip markers feature a broad, flat point, allowing for versatile line widths ideal for both bold writing and shading. Bullet tip markers offer a rounded point, perfect for precise details and fine lines. Jumbo chisel tips are larger versions that create broader strokes, suitable for more significant marking tasks. Each type serves different needs in art, labeling, and professional applications, catering to various user preferences and requirements.</p></p>
