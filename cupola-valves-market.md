<p><p>The Cupola Valves market encompasses various actuation types, including manual, electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Manual valves require human operation, often used in simple applications. Electric valves utilize electric motors for precise control, suitable for automated systems. Pneumatic valves use compressed air for quick response times, making them ideal for process automation. Hydraulic valves rely on liquid pressure to operate, offering high force capabilities for heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs across various industries.</p></p>
