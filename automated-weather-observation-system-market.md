<p><p>The Automated Weather Observation System (AWOS) market is categorized into two main types: Surface Weather and High Altitude Weather systems. Surface Weather systems focus on collecting atmospheric data at ground level, providing critical information for aviation, transportation, and local weather forecasts. High Altitude Weather systems, on the other hand, monitor atmospheric conditions at elevated altitudes, serving applications like air traffic management and meteorological research. Both types are essential for enhancing safety, efficiency, and accuracy in weather-dependent activities.</p></p>
