<p><p>The Organic Bread Flour market comprises primarily two types: machine milled flour and stone ground flour. Machine milled flour is produced using advanced machinery, resulting in a fine, consistent texture ideal for a variety of baking applications. In contrast, stone ground flour is crafted using traditional methods, where grains are crushed between stones, preserving more nutrients and flavor, leading to a coarser texture. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, focusing on quality, nutrition, and baking performance.</p></p>
