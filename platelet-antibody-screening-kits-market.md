<p><ul><li>Antibody Screening</li><li>Crossmatching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platelet antibody screening kits are essential in transfusion medicine for identifying antibodies against platelets. The market is categorized into two main types: Antibody Screening and Crossmatching. Antibody screening involves testing donor or recipient serum to detect specific platelet antibodies, ensuring compatibility before transfusion. Crossmatching, on the other hand, pairs donor platelets with recipient sera to confirm compatibility, minimizing the risk of transfusion reactions. Both types are crucial for safe platelet transfusions and optimizing patient outcomes in clinical settings.</p></p>
