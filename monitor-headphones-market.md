<p><ul><li>Dynamic Headphones</li><li>Moving Iron Headphones</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monitor headphones are categorized primarily into dynamic headphones, moving iron headphones, and other types. Dynamic headphones utilize a diaphragm and voice coil to produce sound, offering a warm tonal quality and wide frequency response, ideal for various listening settings. Moving iron headphones, on the other hand, feature a miniature armature that delivers precise sound reproduction, often favored in professional audio applications for their clarity. Other market types may include electrostatic and planarmagnetic headphones, each offering unique sound characteristics tailored for audiophiles and professionals.</p></p>
