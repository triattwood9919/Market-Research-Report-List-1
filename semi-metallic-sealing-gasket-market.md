<p><p>The semi-metallic sealing gasket market is categorized into two primary types: round and non-circular gaskets. Round gaskets are commonly used in pipe flanges and equipment seals due to their uniform pressure distribution and compatibility with standard flanges. Non-circular gaskets, which include shapes like oval, rectangular, or customized designs, are often used in specialized applications where standard shapes do not fit, providing enhanced sealing capabilities for unique equipment and environments. Both types offer durability and adaptability in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
