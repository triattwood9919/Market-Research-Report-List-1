<p><strong>The Automotive Seat Belt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ALR Three Point Seatbelt</li><li>ELR Three Point Seatbelt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat belt market is primarily categorized into two main types: the ALR (Automatic Locking Retractor) three-point seatbelt and the ELR (Emergency Locking Retractor) three-point seatbelt. ALR seatbelts automatically lock when pulled out to secure the occupant in place, providing added safety during certain situations, such as child restraint installation. In contrast, ELR seatbelts allow free movement during regular driving but engage and lock upon sudden deceleration or impact, ensuring critical protection in crash scenarios.</p></p>
