<p><p>The polycarbonate (PC) plastics market encompasses various manufacturing processes, including injection molding, blow molding, and extrusion molding. Injection molding is used for creating complex shapes with high precision, ideal for automotive and electronics components. Blow molding produces hollow items, perfect for containers and bottles. Extrusion molding shapes PC into continuous profiles, widely utilized in construction and signage. Each method leverages the durability and transparency of polycarbonate, catering to diverse applications across multiple industries, ensuring flexibility in design and production.</p></p>
