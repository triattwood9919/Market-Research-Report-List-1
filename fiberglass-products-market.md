<p><p>The fiberglass products market primarily includes rovings and yarns, which are essential reinforcements in composite applications. Rovings consist of bundles of glass fibers, ideal for manufacturing large-scale components like boat hulls and wind turbine blades due to their strength and lightweight properties. Yarns, made from continuous strands of glass fibers, are used in textiles and composite materials that require flexibility and strength, such as in automotive and construction sectors. Both types enhance durability and performance across various industries.</p></p>
