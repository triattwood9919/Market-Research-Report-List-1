<p><p>The oncology molecular diagnostics market encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)** enables rapid amplification of DNA, aiding in tumor detection. </p><p>2. **INAAT (Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology)** provides simpler, quicker testing without temperature cycling. </p><p>3. **DNA Sequencing** and **NGS (Next-Generation Sequencing)** offer detailed genomic insights, crucial for personalized medicine. </p><p>4. **In Situ Hybridization** visualizes specific RNA or DNA sequences directly in tissue samples. </p><p>5. **DNA Microarrays** allow simultaneous analysis of thousands of genes. </p><p>6. **Other market** includes emerging technologies and hybrid methods for oncology diagnostics.</p></p>
