<p><strong>The Integrated Graphics Processing Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Independent Integrated Graphics Processing Unit</li><li>Hybrid Integrated Graphics Processing Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Graphics Processing Unit (IGPU) market is divided into two main types: Independent Integrated Graphics Processing Units and Hybrid Integrated Graphics Processing Units. Independent IGUs are self-contained graphics solutions built into a processor, optimizing performance for casual gaming and multimedia tasks without the need for separate hardware. On the other hand, Hybrid IGUs combine integrated graphics with dedicated GPUs, dynamically switching between them to provide a balance of power efficiency and enhanced performance, catering to users with varying graphics needs.</p></p>
