<p><ul><li>Refracting Telescope</li><li>Reflector Telescope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The binocular telescope market is primarily categorized into two types: refracting telescopes and reflecting telescopes. Refracting telescopes use lenses to converge light, providing clear images with minimal distortion, making them ideal for observing celestial bodies. Conversely, reflecting telescopes utilize mirrors to gather and focus light, allowing for larger apertures and enhanced light-gathering capabilities. Each type has unique advantages, catering to different preferences and applications among amateur astronomers and professionals, influencing their market demand and growth trajectories.</p></p>
