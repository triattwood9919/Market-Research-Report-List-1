<p><p>The aluminum windows and doors market encompasses two primary types: solid core and hollow core. Solid core aluminum products are constructed from a dense aluminum material, offering enhanced durability, thermal insulation, and security, making them ideal for commercial and high-end residential applications. In contrast, hollow core aluminum products feature a lightweight design with a hollow interior, providing cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. This type offers adequate performance for residential use while being more affordable, catering to budget-conscious consumers.</p></p>
