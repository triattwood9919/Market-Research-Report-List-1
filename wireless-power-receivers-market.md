<p><strong>The Wireless Power Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li><li>Capacitive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless power receivers market consists primarily of two types: inductive coupling and capacitive coupling wireless power receivers. Inductive coupling uses electromagnetic fields to transfer energy between coils, commonly found in devices like smartphones and electric toothbrushes. In contrast, capacitive coupling relies on electric fields to transmit power through capacitive plates, suitable for applications that require efficiency in smaller form factors. Both technologies are pivotal in the growing sectors of consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial automation, enabling seamless charging solutions.</p></p>
