<p><strong>The Low Pressure Galvanized Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Dip Galvanized Pipe</li><li>Cold Galvanized Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Pressure Galvanized Pipe market consists mainly of two types: Hot Dip Galvanized (HDG) and Cold Galvanized Pipes. HDG pipes are coated by immersing them in molten zinc, providing superior corrosion resistance and durability, ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, Cold Galvanized Pipes are coated using electroplating, resulting in a thinner zinc layer, which offers moderate protection and is typically used for indoor plumbing. Both types cater to various industries, including construction, agriculture, and water supply systems.</p></p>
