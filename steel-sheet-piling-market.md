<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Steel Sheet Piles</li><li>Cold-formed steel sheet pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel sheet piling market comprises primarily two types: hot-rolled and cold-formed steel sheet piles. Hot-rolled steel sheet piles are manufactured by heating steel and then rolling it into shape, resulting in strong, durable profiles ideal for deep foundations and heavy load applications. In contrast, cold-formed steel sheet piles are produced at room temperature, offering lighter profiles with precise dimensions. Cold-formed options are commonly used in construction projects where weight and ease of installation are critical, providing versatility in various applications.</p></p>
