<p><p>Hotel management WiFi software is categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed directly on the hotel's servers, offering greater control and customization, but may require significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted remotely, enabling easy scalability, automatic updates, and lower initial costs. This solution provides flexibility and accessibility, allowing hotel staff to manage WiFi services from anywhere, enhancing guest experience without the burden of hardware management.</p></p>
