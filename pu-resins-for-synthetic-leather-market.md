<p><strong>The PU Resins for Synthetic Leather Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-process Synthetic Leather</li><li>Wet-process Synthetic Leather</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PU resins are crucial in the synthetic leather market, employed in two main processes: dry-process and wet-process synthetic leather. Dry-process synthetic leather utilizes PU resins in a solvent-free method, resulting in a lightweight and durable material, ideal for fashion and automotive applications. In contrast, wet-process synthetic leather involves immersing fabric substrates in PU resin solutions, yielding a more flexible and soft texture, often used for upholstery and garment manufacturing. Both methods provide environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional leather.</p></p>
