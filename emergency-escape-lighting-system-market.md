<p><p>The Emergency Escape Lighting System market comprises two primary types: Central Emergency Lighting and Self-contained Emergency Lighting. Central Emergency Lighting systems are centrally powered, utilizing a centralized battery and control system to provide illumination during emergencies across multiple areas. In contrast, Self-contained Emergency Lighting units operate independently with their own batteries, ensuring operation without reliance on external power sources. These systems cater to diverse applications, enhancing safety by ensuring visibility and guiding occupants during evacuation in critical situations.</p></p>
