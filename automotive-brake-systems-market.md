<p><ul><li>Antilock Braking System (ABS)</li><li>Traction Control System (TCS)</li><li>Electronic Stability Control (ESC)</li><li>Electronic Brake-Force Distribution (EBD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive brake systems market includes various advanced technologies enhancing vehicle safety and performance. Antilock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during hard braking, improving control. Traction Control System (TCS) manages wheel spin during acceleration, optimizing grip. Electronic Stability Control (ESC) enhances vehicle stability by detecting and reducing loss of traction. Electronic Brake-Force Distribution (EBD) adjusts brake force between front and rear wheels based on load conditions, ensuring efficient braking. Together, these systems significantly enhance driving safety and vehicle handling.</p></p>
