<p><strong>The Brake Calipers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake caliper market is primarily segmented into single piston and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize one piston to apply pressure evenly across the brake pads, ideal for lighter vehicles and cost-sensitive applications. In contrast, multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons, allowing for improved braking performance, better heat dissipation, and enhanced pad wear, making them suitable for high-performance and heavier vehicles. Each type serves distinct market demands, influencing design, efficiency, and overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
