<p><strong>The Ultra-capacitor for Electric Bus Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Double Layer Capacitor</li><li>Hybrid Capacitor</li><li>Pseudo Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultra-capacitor market for electric buses includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Electrochemical Double Layer Capacitors (EDLC)**: These store energy through electrostatic charge separation, offering high power density and rapid charging/discharging capabilities, ideal for quick energy bursts.</p><p>2. **Hybrid Capacitors**: Combining features of EDLCs and batteries, these provide both high energy and power density, suitable for applications needing sustained power with good cycle life.</p><p>3. **Pseudo Capacitors**: Utilizing both electrostatic and electrochemical charge storage, these offer higher energy densities than EDLCs, making them suitable for applications requiring longer energy supply duration.</p></p>
