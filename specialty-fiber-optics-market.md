<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Singlemode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Specialty Fiber Optics market is primarily divided into two types: multimode fiber and singlemode fiber. Multimode fiber, with a larger core, allows multiple light modes to propagate, making it suitable for short-distance communication like data centers and enterprise networks. In contrast, singlemode fiber has a smaller core, enabling only one mode of light, which facilitates long-distance transmission with higher bandwidth and lower signal loss, ideal for telecommunications and wide-area networks. Both types cater to diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
