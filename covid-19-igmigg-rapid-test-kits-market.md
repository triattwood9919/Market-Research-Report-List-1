<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM/IgG rapid test kits market is segmented into three main types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs offer quick, point-of-care results, making them suitable for widespread screening. ELISA tests are more sensitive and specific, typically used in laboratories for confirming infections. Neutralization assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, providing insights into immune response and vaccine efficacy. Each type plays a distinct role in COVID-19 diagnosis and research.</p></p>
