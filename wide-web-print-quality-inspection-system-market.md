<p><p>The Wide Web Print Quality Inspection System market is divided into two main types: in-line and off-line systems. In-line Print Quality Inspection Systems are integrated into the printing process, allowing real-time monitoring and immediate error correction to ensure high-quality output. Conversely, Off-line Print Quality Inspection Systems operate independently of the printing process, where printed materials are assessed after production, enabling detailed analysis and quality control. Both types are essential for maintaining standards in high-speed print operations.</p></p>
