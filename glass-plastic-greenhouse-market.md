<p><p>The greenhouse market is divided into two main types: glass greenhouses and plastic greenhouses. Glass greenhouses offer superior light transmission, durability, and aesthetics, making them suitable for high-end horticulture and commercial applications. However, they are generally more expensive and require robust structures. In contrast, plastic greenhouses, often made from polycarbonate or polyethylene, are lightweight, cost-effective, and easier to install. They provide good insulation and UV protection, making them popular among small-scale growers and hobbyists seeking affordable solutions.</p></p>
