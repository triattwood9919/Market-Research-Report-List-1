<p><strong>The Non-invasive Intracranial Pressure (ICP) Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sound Signal Detection</li><li>Blood Flow Signal Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-invasive Intracranial Pressure (ICP) Monitoring Devices market consists of two primary types: sound signal detection and blood flow signal detection. Sound signal detection utilizes acoustic methods to gauge pressure changes within the cranial cavity, providing real-time data without the need for surgical intervention. Blood flow signal detection, on the other hand, analyzes the hemodynamic response and alterations in cerebral blood flow to infer ICP levels. Both methods aim to enhance patient safety and comfort while enabling effective monitoring of intracranial conditions.</p></p>
