<p><p>The asthma devices market primarily includes inhalers and nebulizers. Inhalers, such as metered-dose inhalers and dry powder inhalers, deliver medication directly to the lungs in a portable form, allowing for quick relief from asthma symptoms. Nebulizers, on the other hand, convert liquid medication into a mist, making it easier for patients, especially young children, to inhale. Both devices are essential for managing asthma, ensuring effective delivery of bronchodilators and anti-inflammatory medications to maintain respiratory health.</p></p>
