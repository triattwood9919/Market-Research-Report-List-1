<p><p>The Liquid Applied Roofing Market encompasses various types, including Bituminous Membranes, Elastomeric Membranes, and Cementitious Membranes. Bituminous membranes are typically made from asphalt and provide excellent waterproofing and durability. Elastomeric membranes are flexible, offering good resistance to UV rays and temperature fluctuations. Meanwhile, Cementitious membranes, often made from a mix of cement and polymers, provide a rigid but durable solution, ideal for flat roofs. Together, these options cater to diverse building needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
