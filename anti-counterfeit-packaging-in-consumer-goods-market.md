<p><p>Anti-counterfeit packaging in the consumer goods market involves two primary types: authentication and track and trace packaging technologies. Authentication packaging technology utilizes features like holograms, QR codes, and unique serial numbers to verify the product's legitimacy at the point of sale. Track and trace packaging technology enables the monitoring of a product's journey through the supply chain using RFID tags or barcodes, ensuring transparency and accountability. Together, these technologies help combat counterfeiting and enhance consumer trust in brand integrity.</p></p>
