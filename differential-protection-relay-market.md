<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Differential Protection Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Differential Relay</li><li>Biased or Percentage Differential Relay</li><li>Voltage Balance Differential Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The differential protection relay market consists of several types designed to enhance electrical system reliability. Current differential relays compare the input and output currents of electrical equipment, ensuring quick fault identification. Biased or percentage differential relays use a biasing mechanism to accommodate normal variations in load, providing protection against inrush currents. Voltage balance differential relays monitor voltage differences, ensuring proper operation during faults. Each type addresses specific protection needs and optimizes system safety and efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
