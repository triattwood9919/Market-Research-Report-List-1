<p><p>The modular belt conveyor market is primarily divided into two types: open belt conveyors and enclosed belt conveyors. Open belt conveyors feature a design that allows for unrestricted airflow and visibility, making them suitable for transporting bulk materials in various industrial settings. In contrast, enclosed belt conveyors are designed with a protective cover, ensuring safe transport of materials while minimizing dust and spillage. Both types are utilized in diverse industries, enhancing material handling efficiency and safety.</p></p>
